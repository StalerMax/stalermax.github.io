NOBODY

Nobody is a platformer and puzzle game developed for the Games Fleadh 2019.

To solve the challenging puzzles of the game you will need to kill the enemies of every level and use the unique body change mechanic to reach places you could not otherwise.

Platform: Browser | PC
Project length: 3 weeks


PLAY IN BROWSER

OVERVIEW

When I created Nobody I wanted to explore new gameplay ideas that I had never seen before and thought could be interesting in a platformer/puzzle game.

I always loved puzzle games like Braid, Limbo, Portal, etc... And Nobody was my chance to create my first game of this genre putting to the test my level design skills.

The main goal of this project was to design puzzles that could give great satisfaction to the players when solving them and offer the users a challenging and unique experience.

WHAT I DID

• Created all the art assets using pixel art style.

• Created all the sound effects (but the music).

• Designed all puzzles and levels of the game.

• Designed the gameplay including the body change mechanich.

• Coded a glow shader to add a futuristic style to the game.
